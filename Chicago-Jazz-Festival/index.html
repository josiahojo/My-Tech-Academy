The oldest of Chicago's free lakefront music festivals boasts an incredibly diverse lineup, which includes free neighborhood concerts leading up to the main show at Millennnium Park each August. The range of artisits comprising the Chicago Jazz Festival runs the gamut from Jazz legends to influential mondern masters and crucial new voices in the genre's continuing evolution.
